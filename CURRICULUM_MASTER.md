# KOREAN LEARNING CURRICULUM - 6 MONTH INTENSIVE
## Daewon's Path to Conversational Fluency

**Duration**: 26 Weeks (December 17, 2025 - June 17, 2026)
**Target Level**: Conversational Fluency (TOPIK 3-4 Equivalent)
**Learning Method**: Intensive Daily Study + Immersion

---

## Overview

### Three Phases of Learning

```
Phase 1: Foundation Building (Weeks 1-8)
├── Goal: Master core grammar and 1000 vocabulary
├── Focus: Basic communication structures
└── Output: Simple conversations and writing

Phase 2: Intermediate Expansion (Weeks 9-17)
├── Goal: Expand to 2500 vocabulary, complex grammar
├── Focus: Natural expression and comprehension
└── Output: Extended conversations and paragraphs

Phase 3: Advanced Fluency (Weeks 18-26)
├── Goal: Achieve fluency with 3500-4000 vocabulary
├── Focus: Nuance, idioms, and authentic materials
└── Output: Natural Korean communication
```

## PHASE 1: FOUNDATION BUILDING (Weeks 1-8)

### Week-by-Week Breakdown

#### **WEEK 1: Basics & Present Tense**
**Vocabulary Target**: 120 words
**Grammar**: Introduction to Korean sentence structure

**Day 1: Numbers & Basic Greetings**
- Numbers 1-100 (sino-Korean and native Korean)
- Essential greetings: 안녕하세요, 안녕, 감사합니다, 죄송합니다
- Self-introduction basics: 저는 ___ 입니다

**Day 2: Subject & Topic Particles (이/가, 은/는)**
- When to use each particle
- Difference between topic and subject
- 10 practice sentences

**Day 3: Object Particle (을/를)**
- Object marking
- Sentence structure: Subject + Object + Verb
- Integration with previous particles

**Day 4: Present Tense (Action Verbs)**
- -아/어요 conjugation rules
- 20 common action verbs (먹다, 가다, 오다, 보다, etc.)
- Practice sentences

**Day 5: Present Tense (Descriptive Verbs/Adjectives)**
- Using descriptive verbs as adjectives and predicates
- 15 common descriptive verbs (크다, 작다, 좋다, 나쁘다, etc.)
- Comparison with action verbs

**Day 6: Review & Practice**
- Written exercises combining all week's grammar
- Speaking prompts for native practice
- Anki deck review

**Day 7: Weekly Assessment**
- 20 vocabulary quiz
- 10 grammar questions
- 1 short writing (50 characters)
- Update progress tracker

#### **WEEK 2: Time, Location & Existence**
**Vocabulary Target**: 130 words

**Day 4: Basic Question Formation**
- Question words: 뭐, 누구, 어디, 언제, 왜, 어떻게

**Day 5: More Common Verbs & Daily Activities**
- 30 verbs for daily life: 자다, 일어나다, 씻다, 입다, etc.

### Morning Session (45-60 min)
1. Anki review (20-30 min)

### Afternoon Session (60-90 min)
1. Grammar lesson (30 min)

### Evening Session (30-45 min)
1. Speaking practice with native friend (variable)

## Integration with Daily Practice

### Podcast Listening Strategy
- **Weeks 1-4**: Passive listening, get used to sounds
- **Weeks 5-8**: Active listening, try to catch known words
- **Weeks 9-13**: Listen with transcripts, study unknown words
- **Weeks 14-17**: Listen without transcripts, test comprehension
- **Weeks 18-26**: Engage with variety of content, near-native listening

### Native Speaker Practice Integration
- **Weeks 1-4**: Practice basic patterns taught in lessons
- **Weeks 5-8**: Tell stories, describe daily life
- **Weeks 9-13**: Discuss topics, ask for feedback
- **Weeks 14-17**: Complex conversations, cultural topics
- **Weeks 18-26**: Natural free-flowing conversations

### Anki Deck Strategy
- Daily reviews: 100-150 cards
- New cards: 20-30/day
- Format: Korean sentence → English + Korean word highlighted
- Tags by week and topic
