## Why Anki for Korean?

Spaced Repetition System (SRS) is THE most effective way to memorize vocabulary long-term. Anki ensures you review words right before you forget them, maximizing retention with minimal time.

**Statistics**: 
- Regular review: ~30% retention after 1 month
- Anki SRS: ~80-90% retention after 1 month

## Card Creation Process

### Option 1: Manual Creation (Recommended for beginners)
1. Open Anki
2. Click "Add" (or press 'A')
3. Select deck (e.g., "Week_01_Foundation")
4. Enter front and back as shown above
5. Add tags: `week1`, `noun`, `high-frequency`
6. Click "Add"

### Option 2: Bulk Import (For efficiency)
1. Create CSV file with vocabulary
2. Format: `Korean Sentence | English + Details | Tags`
3. Import into Anki
4. Map fields correctly

### From Naver Dictionary:
1. Look up word on Naver
2. Copy example sentence
3. Copy definition
4. Add pronunciation
5. Create card in Anki

## Optimal Anki Settings

### New Cards Tab:
- **New cards/day**: 20-30 (Week 1-4), 15-20 (later weeks)
- **Learning steps**: 1m 10m 1h 6h
  - See card 4 times before graduation
- **Graduating interval**: 3 days
- **Easy interval**: 4 days
- **Starting ease**: 250%
- **Order**: Show new cards in order added

### Reviews Tab:
- **Maximum reviews/day**: 9999 (unlimited)
  - *NEVER skip due reviews!*
- **Easy bonus**: 130%
- **Interval modifier**: 100%
- **Maximum interval**: 365 days

### Lapses Tab:
- **Steps**: 10m 1h 6h
- **New interval**: 20%
- **Minimum interval**: 1 day
- **Leech threshold**: 8 lapses
- **Leech action**: Tag only (don't suspend)

## Grading System (1-2-3-4)

### How to Grade Cards:

**Again (1)** - Red:
- Couldn't remember at all
- Completely forgot
- Wrong answer
â†’ Card will show again soon (10 min)

**Hard (2)** - Gray:
- Struggled to remember
- Took long time
- Barely got it
â†’ Shorter interval than "Good"

**Good (3)** - Green:
- Remembered correctly
- Took a few seconds
- Normal recall
â†’ Standard SRS interval

**Easy (4)** - Blue:
- Instant recognition
- No effort needed
- Too easy
â†’ Much longer interval

### General Rule:
- **80% of time**: Press "Good" (3)
- **15% of time**: Press "Again" (1) or "Hard" (2)
- **5% of time**: Press "Easy" (4)

**NEVER** press "Easy" just to reduce reviews. Only if truly instant.

## Troubleshooting

### "Too many reviews piling up!"
- **Solution**: Do ALL reviews daily, reduce new cards temporarily
- Never let backlog grow > 100

### "Cards are too easy"
- **Solution**: Increase new cards/day
- Check interval modifier (might increase)

### "Cards are too hard"
- **Solution**: Review previous lessons
- Add more context to cards
- Break down complex words

### "Forgetting everything"
- **Solution**: Learning steps might be too aggressive
- Increase review frequency
- Make sure you understand, not just memorize

### "This takes too long!"
- **Solution**: You're doing too many new cards
- Focus on quality over quantity
- 15 well-learned words > 50 barely-seen words

## Statistics to Track

### Check Weekly:
- **Retention Rate**: Aim for 85-90%
  - If lower: Review more carefully, slower new cards
  - If higher: Might be pressing "Good" too liberally

- **Average Review Time**: Aim for 5-10 seconds/card
  - If longer: Cards too complicated, simplify

- **Cards Due Tomorrow**: Should be manageable
  - If too many: Reduce new cards

### Check Monthly:
- **Total Cards**: Should align with curriculum
  - Month 1: ~500 cards
  - Month 2: ~1000 cards
  - Month 6: ~4000 cards

- **Mature Cards**: Should be growing
  - Mature = interval > 21 days
  - These are "learned" words

## Anki Shortcuts (Save Time!)

- **Space**: Show answer / Rate "Good"
- **1**: Rate "Again"
- **2**: Rate "Hard"
- **3**: Rate "Good"
- **4**: Rate "Easy"
- **A**: Add new card
- **E**: Edit current card
- **@**: Suspend card
- **Ctrl+Z**: Undo rating

Learn these! They make reviews much faster.
